Oreo: The TypeScript-like evolution of Luau - A familiar syntax for Luau developers seeking a TypeScript-like experience.

Oreo is an evolution of Luau designed to offer a TypeScript-like syntax, similar to what you might find in RBXTS. This enhancement aims to make development more intuitive and streamlined for those familiar with TypeScript.

Although Oreo is still in its early stages, it provides a new approach to Luau, making the transition smoother for developers accustomed to TypeScript. Check out Oreo and see how it can simplify your development process.
